<objective>
Adopt `SubcategoryPicker` in the two **fill-field** surfaces (D-07, R-UP-05/R-UP-06): the create/edit expense form (`expense-form-dialog`) and the create transaction form (`transaction-form-dialog`). Here the picker does NOT commit — tapping a subcategory fills the form's hidden `subCategoryId` field (and a visible label) and the existing form submit button persists the record. Replace the cascading Categoria to Sottocategoria `Select` pair with a single "Categorizza" trigger that opens the picker.

Purpose: Eliminate the cascading Select pairs from the two manual-entry forms in favor of the unified picker, while keeping the form-submit lifecycle intact (this is fill-field, not commit-on-tap).
Output: Both form dialogs use the picker; the cascading Selects are gone from these two files. Old picker components are deleted later in 39-06.

Note on wave: this plan also edits `expenses/page.tsx` / `transactions/page.tsx` (to pass `mostUsed` to the form dialogs), which 39-03 also edits — hence it runs in wave 4 after 39-03 to avoid a same-wave file conflict.
</objective>
