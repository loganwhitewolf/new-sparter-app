<objective>
Land the database schema change that introduces the `flow_nature` enum and the `nature` column on both `sub_category` and `user_subcategory_override`, drop the `custom_name` NOT NULL constraint in the SAME migration (per RESEARCH key finding #1 / Pitfall 6), and populate every system subcategory in `scripts/seed-data.ts` with a default nature (per ADR-0003 and D-09).

Purpose: This is the foundation for the entire phase. Plan 37-03 (DAL nature trend) and Plan 37-05 (settings override) both depend on the schema being in place. The `customName` NULL relaxation is mandatory — without it, nature-only override upserts will violate the NOT NULL constraint.
