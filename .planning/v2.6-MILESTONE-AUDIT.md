raw bank description instead of its group title. Every other surface in the app shows
the group title. Fix is small and mirrors the already-shipped group-title join in
`lib/dal/transactions.ts`: add the `expenseGroupMembership`/`expenseGroup` leftJoin to
`getCategoryDetail`'s `topTransactionRows` query and prefer `groupTitle` in the
display-title fallback.
