## Tech Debt

### ⚠ WARNING: Misleading `ImportFormatRow` comment (lib/dal/import-formats.ts)

**Phase:** 59-import-wizard-attach-format  
**Files:** `lib/dal/import-formats.ts` lines 19, 31, 52  
**Severity:** Warning (zero runtime risk)  
**Description:** The type comment at line 31 reads: "Identity fields sourced from platform (proposedByUserId replaces ownerUserId; visibility dropped)". The `visibility: string` field at line 19 is projected from `importFormatVersion.visibility` (not `platform.visibility`, which was dropped in Phase 58). The comment makes it appear that `visibility` was removed or is a platform field — a future developer could mistakenly drop it from the SELECT.  
**Risk:** No current bug. Future confusion only.  
**Suggested fix:** One-line comment correction: clarify that `visibility` is `importFormatVersion.visibility` (format-level, always present).
