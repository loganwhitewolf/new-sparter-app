<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <name>Task 2: Human verifies the movers drill-down end-to-end</name>
  <what-built>
    Per-month movers drill-down on `/dashboard/overview`: clicking a month's bar highlights that month's Entrate + Uscite bars and updates an inline panel below the chart. The panel splits movers into red "Dove hai speso di più" and green "Dove hai risparmiato", reads as Italian sentences, defaults to the last month with data on load, and shows an empty state for the first month.
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    1. Start the app (`yarn dev`) and open `http://localhost:3000/dashboard/overview` (log in if needed). Pick a year with several months of data via the year-selector pill.
    2. MOVE-04 (default): On first load, confirm the movers panel is ALREADY visible below the chart, and the highlighted (full-opacity) bars correspond to the LAST month that actually has transactions — NOT necessarily December. All other months' bars should be dimmed (~40% opacity).
    3. MOVE-01 (click → highlight + panel): Click a different month's bar (either the green Entrate or red Uscite bar). Confirm that month's two bars go to full opacity, the rest dim, and the panel heading + contents update to the clicked month. There may be a brief subtle loading state inside the panel (not a full-page spinner).
    4. MOVE-02 (two sections, hide empty): Confirm the panel shows a red "Dove hai speso di più" section and/or a green "Dove hai risparmiato" section. If a month only has increases (or only decreases), confirm the empty section is HIDDEN, not shown empty.
    5. MOVE-03 (humanized copy): Confirm each line reads like "{Categoria} · €{importo} in più" or "… in meno", and that a brand-new category shows "{Categoria} · spesa nuova". Confirm there are NO percentages and NO "before → after" arrows anywhere.
    6. Heading (D-02): Confirm the panel heading reads "{Mese} {Anno} vs {Mese precedente} {Anno precedente}", e.g. "Maggio 2025 vs Aprile 2025". For January, the previous month should be "Dicembre" of the prior year.
    7. MOVE-05 (empty state): Click the FIRST available month's bar (leftmost month with data / January). Confirm the panel shows the empty-state message ("Nessuna variazione significativa — mese di partenza o nessuna spesa sopra €15.") instead of mover lists, because there is no prior month to compare.
    8. Sync check: Confirm the chart's highlighted month and the panel's heading month ALWAYS agree — they should never drift apart.
    9. URL check: Confirm clicking bars does NOT change the `?year=` URL param (month selection is ephemeral).
  </how-to-verify>
  <resume-signal>Type "approved" if all 8 checks pass, or describe the specific failures (which MOVE item, what you saw vs expected) to route a fix back into Plan 02.</resume-signal>
