<objective>
Render the "Suggerimenti tag" block inside the existing post-import summary screen (D-08b): one
pre-checked checklist per non-archived date-ranged tag with at least one un-tagged match in range.

Purpose: this is the second of D-08's two suggestion triggers, and the one users actually see most
often — every subsequent import re-surfaces "did you mean to add this to your Sharm 2026 tag?"
without requiring the user to remember to check `/settings/tags` manually. It shares no files with
Plan 67-08 (different page, different component) and depends only on Plan 67-05's already-tested
`computeAllTagSuggestions`/`confirmTagSuggestionAction`.

Output: a new `TagSuggestionSection` component and an extended `/import/[fileId]/suggestions` page.
</objective>
