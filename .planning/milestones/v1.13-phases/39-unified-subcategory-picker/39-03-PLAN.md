<objective>
Adopt the new `SubcategoryPicker` across the four **commit-on-tap** categorization surfaces (D-07, R-UP-05/R-UP-06): single-expense categorize (`expense-categorize-dialog`, shared by the Expenses page AND the Transactions table), bulk categorize (`bulk-categorize-dialog`), and onboarding Step 4 (`subcategory-combobox`). Purpose: Replace `CategoryCombobox` (Popover+Command) and the onboarding `SubcategoryCombobox` at the categorize call sites with the single unified picker. This plan does NOT delete the old components (39-06 does) — it only stops using them here.
Output: Four commit-on-tap surfaces wired to `SubcategoryPicker`, with `getMostUsedSubcategories` threaded through the Expenses, Transactions, and Onboarding pages.
</objective>
